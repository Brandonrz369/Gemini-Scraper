About Us
At SoundCloud, we empower artists and creators to share their music and connect with fans around the world. We’re looking for a talented Web Developer to join our dynamic team and help us enhance our platform.

🔹 Job Description
As a Web Developer at SoundCloud, you will be responsible for building and maintaining our web applications, ensuring a seamless user experience for millions of users. You will collaborate with designers and product managers to implement new features and improve existing ones.

📌 Key Responsibilities:
Develop and maintain web applications using modern web technologies.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and implement new features.
Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
Troubleshoot and debug issues to ensure optimal performance.
Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends.

🎯 Qualifications:
Proven experience as a Web Developer with a strong portfolio of projects.
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React or Angular.
Familiarity with server-side languages (e.g., Node.js, PHP).
Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git).
Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

💰 Salary & Compensation:
Competitive salary based on experience.
Additional benefits, including health insurance, flexible working hours, and opportunities for professional development.

Join us at SoundCloud and help shape the future of music! 🚀
